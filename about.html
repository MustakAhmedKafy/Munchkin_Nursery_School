About

Welcome to Munchkin Academy, a brand-new nursery that has opened in the heart of Sundon park offering 50 OFSTED registered places for children between 3months to 5years.

We are open from 8.00pm to 6.00pm, Monday to Friday 52 weeks a year excluding bank holidays. We aim to provide flexible, affordable, quality childcare to support families in our community.

Our setting provides places for private paying families and families who would like a funded place of 15 or 30 hours.

We are a multi-cultural nursery school that welcome’s children of all cultural backgrounds. We have a committed, qualified, friendly team who care and support the learning opportunities for the needs of the individual child.
